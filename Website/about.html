About MMC

Manila Mission Control (MMC) was developed by an inspired group of developers from the Philippines who were interested in helping NASA improve their relations amongst the Filipino people.


The developers, and NASA, intended MMC to be the "starting point" for budding astronomers within the Philippines. They envisioned it as a "control center" where you can launch your imagination into the heavens above.


In MMC, you can find various NASA resources, as well as other helpful information from NASA to pique your interest in the exploration of the unknown. You can get a first-hand view of what the ISS looks like from the inside in the homepage, and there are other VR videos in the VR section.


More features are to be added soon, so stay tuned for more information and updates on this site. Until then, fly high, pilots and astronauts!
